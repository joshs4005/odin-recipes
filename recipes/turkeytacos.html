Turkey Tacos

Description

Turkey Tacos are a delicious and healthy alternative to beef tacos. In this recipe you will see just how easy and delicious turkey tacos can be for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • ground turkey
  • taco shells
  • mexican cheese blend
  • taco seasoning
  • salsa

Steps

  1. Cook ground turkey in pan until no longer pink, add liquid and taco seasoning after
  2. Reduce heat and allow to simmer 10 minutes
  3. Fill taco shells with your cooked ground turkey and top with cheese
  4. Preheat oven to 400 degrees and bake tacos in oven for 8 minutes or until top edges of shell turn brown
  5. Allow to cool and add extra cheese if desired
  6. Add salsa on top for a nice added kick
  7. Enjoy!
