Pizza

Description

Making a homemade pizza might seem hard but it really isn't as long as you have a few key ingredients and the right utensils needed. Let's look at how easy it is to make and how delicious something made at home can be!

Ingredients

  • pre-made pizza dough
  • pepperonis
  • mozzarrella cheese
  • butter
  • garlic
  • red pepper flakes
  • ranch dressing

Steps

  1. roll dough in to circle
  2. flatten middle, create desired crust
  3. generously spread mozzarella cheese throughout the middle of the dough up to the crust
  4. add pepperonis on top of cheese
  5. butter crust
  6. sprinkle garlic on top of pizza including crust
  7. place pizza in oven
  8. bake at 425 degrees for 15-18 minutes untli crust is golden brown
  9. take pizza out of oven and allow to cool
  10. sprinkle red pepper flakes on top of pizza
  11. cut in slices, add ranch to the side and enjoy!
